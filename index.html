J.A.C.E.

Javascript Ant Chess Engine

What would happen if our beloved Ant System algorithm was applied to a demo other than the classical TSP? Here is a javascript-only chess engine which uses AS to choose the next move from time to time (AS parameters are reset everytime you move as the white player). Every piece ranks its possible moves independently; phereomone trails are placed directly on squares. Obviousy, J.A.C.E is not meant to be a chessmaster, rather a not-so-stupid player who can choose a pretty decent move in more or less one second. See AntChess.js file for full implementation details.
